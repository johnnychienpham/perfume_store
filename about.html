About Aroma

        Aroma is a visionary beauty-retail concept founded in France by Dominique Mandonnaud in 1970. Aroma's unique, open-sell environment features 
        an ever-increasing amount of classic and emerging brands across a broad range of product categories including skincare, makeup, fragrance, body and hair care, 
        in addition to Sephora's own private label.

        Today, Aroma is not only the leading chain of perfume and cosmetics stores in France, but also a powerful beauty presence in countries around the world.

        Owned by LVMH MoÃ«t Hennessy Louis Vuitton, the world's leading luxury goods group, Aroma is highly regarded 
        as a beauty trailblazer, thanks to its unparalleled assortment of prestige products, unbiased service from experts, interactive shopping environment, disruptive 
        spirit and constant innovation.

        Aroma operates approximately 1,900 stores in 29 countries worldwide, with an expanding base of over 200 stores across the Asia Pacific region including 
        Australia, China, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia & India.
        
            
        Aroma operates its retail network on a stand-alone basis. In principle, Aroma does not grant any rights to third parties to operate its store concept and to use 
        the Aroma trademark. However, you can find our current career opportunities on our website.
